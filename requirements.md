# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document specifies the requirements for an AI-assisted diagnostic system designed to serve the healthcare needs of rural India. The system enables patients to receive preliminary diagnoses through AI assistance while maintaining doctor oversight and validation, addressing the shortage of medical professionals in rural areas.

## Glossary

- **System**: The rural healthcare diagnostic platform
- **Patient**: Individual seeking medical diagnosis and treatment
- **Doctor**: Licensed medical professional providing validation and final diagnosis
- **AI_Doctor_Assistant**: AI system that provides preliminary diagnostic suggestions
- **AI_Validate_Assistant**: AI system that validates and cross-checks diagnoses
- **AI_Research_Assistant**: AI system that provides research support to doctors
- **DynamoDB**: Central database managing all diagnostic requests and reports
- **Diagnosis_Report**: Preliminary diagnostic assessment generated by AI
- **Final_Report**: Doctor-validated and approved diagnostic report
- **Updated_Diagnosis**: Revised diagnosis after doctor validation

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Patient Diagnosis Initiation

**User Story:** As a patient in rural India, I want to submit my symptoms and medical concerns to receive preliminary diagnostic assistance, so that I can get timely medical guidance when doctors are not immediately available.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a patient submits symptoms and medical history, THE System SHALL create a new diagnostic request in DynamoDB
2. WHEN a diagnostic request is created, THE System SHALL assign a unique identifier and timestamp
3. WHEN patient information is incomplete, THE System SHALL prompt for required medical details
4. THE System SHALL accept symptom descriptions in local languages and English
5. WHEN a patient submits a request, THE System SHALL provide an estimated wait time for preliminary diagnosis

### Requirement 2: AI-Assisted Preliminary Diagnosis

**User Story:** As a patient, I want to receive AI-generated preliminary diagnostic suggestions based on my symptoms, so that I can understand potential health issues while waiting for doctor validation.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a diagnostic request is processed, THE AI_Doctor_Assistant SHALL analyze symptoms and generate preliminary diagnosis suggestions
2. WHEN generating diagnoses, THE AI_Doctor_Assistant SHALL provide confidence levels for each suggestion
3. THE AI_Doctor_Assistant SHALL include recommended immediate actions and precautions
4. WHEN multiple conditions are possible, THE AI_Doctor_Assistant SHALL rank them by likelihood
5. THE System SHALL generate a Diagnosis_Report containing all preliminary findings

### Requirement 3: Optional Second Opinion Validation

**User Story:** As a patient, I want the option to request a second AI opinion on my doctor-approved diagnosis, so that I can gain additional confidence in my medical assessment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Final_Report is generated by a doctor, THE System SHALL offer patients the option to request AI validation
2. WHEN a patient chooses validation, THE AI_Validate_Assistant SHALL analyze the Final_Report and provide a second opinion
3. THE AI_Validate_Assistant SHALL compare the doctor's diagnosis with its own analysis and flag any significant differences
4. WHEN validation is requested, THE System SHALL present both the original diagnosis and the AI second opinion to the patient
5. THE System SHALL maintain records of validation requests and outcomes for quality improvement

### Requirement 4: Doctor Review and Final Approval

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want to review AI-generated diagnoses with research assistance and provide final medical approval, so that patients receive accurate and professionally validated healthcare guidance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Diagnosis_Report is generated by the AI_Doctor_Assistant, THE System SHALL present it to available doctors for review
2. WHEN a doctor reviews a diagnosis, THE System SHALL provide access to original symptoms, AI analysis, and AI_Research_Assistant support
3. THE Doctor SHALL have the ability to approve or modify the AI-generated diagnosis with additional comments
4. WHEN a doctor modifies a diagnosis, THE System SHALL allow the doctor to add explanatory comments and request patient feedback if needed
5. WHEN a doctor completes review, THE System SHALL generate a Final_Report containing the approved or modified diagnosis
6. THE System SHALL track doctor review time and maintain quality metrics

### Requirement 5: AI Research Assistant Support

**User Story:** As a doctor, I want access to AI-powered research assistance, so that I can make informed decisions with current medical knowledge and evidence.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a doctor requests research support, THE AI_Research_Assistant SHALL provide relevant medical literature and guidelines
2. THE AI_Research_Assistant SHALL search current medical databases and research papers
3. WHEN reviewing complex cases, THE AI_Research_Assistant SHALL suggest differential diagnoses
4. THE System SHALL provide evidence-based treatment recommendations
5. THE AI_Research_Assistant SHALL highlight any drug interactions or contraindications

### Requirement 6: Queue Management and Prioritization

**User Story:** As a healthcare administrator, I want the system to manage diagnostic requests efficiently, so that urgent cases receive priority and resources are optimally utilized.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE DynamoDB SHALL prioritize cases based on symptom severity and urgency indicators
2. WHEN critical symptoms are detected, THE System SHALL escalate cases for immediate attention
3. THE System SHALL balance workload across available AI assistants and doctors
4. WHEN queue capacity is exceeded, THE System SHALL provide realistic wait time estimates
5. THE System SHALL maintain queue statistics and performance metrics

### Requirement 7: Multi-language Support

**User Story:** As a patient in rural India, I want to interact with the system in my local language, so that I can accurately describe my symptoms and understand the diagnosis.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL support Hindi, English, and major regional languages
2. WHEN a patient selects a language, THE System SHALL maintain that language throughout the session
3. THE System SHALL translate medical terminology into patient-friendly language
4. WHEN generating reports, THE System SHALL provide translations in the patient's preferred language
5. THE AI_Doctor_Assistant SHALL understand symptom descriptions in multiple languages

### Requirement 8: Data Security and Privacy

**User Story:** As a patient, I want my medical information to be secure and private, so that my health data is protected according to healthcare privacy standards.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE System SHALL encrypt all patient data in transit and at rest
2. WHEN accessing patient records, THE System SHALL authenticate and authorize users
3. THE System SHALL maintain audit logs of all data access and modifications
4. WHEN sharing data between components, THE System SHALL use secure communication protocols
5. THE System SHALL comply with Indian healthcare data protection regulations
